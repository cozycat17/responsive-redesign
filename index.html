About

For more than a decade, Brown MMA has provided students with comprehensive training in Mixed Martial Arts, alongside a space to create lifelong friendships and engage safely in combat sports. We meet Mondays and Thursdays from 6pm-8pm, and Saturdays 12pm-2pm in Kasper Multipurpose room (located inside Faunce.)
